this game is called "BOB'S BURGERS Clue Game".
the mystery: LINDA BELCHER IS HOSTING A MURDER MYSTERY! SHE'S INVITED BOB, TINA, GENE, LOUISE AND TEDDY, AND INSISTED THEY ALL DRESS UP LIKE DIFFERENT CHARACTERS. SHE TELLS THEM HER FRIEND "NED BODDY" IS RUNNING LATE BECAUSE HE'S BEEN MURDERED SOMEWHERE IN THE APARTMENT! IT'S UP TO YOU TO FIND OUT WHO COULD HAVE DONE THIS? AND WHERE DID THEY DO IT? AND WHAT DID THEY DO IT WITH?
who? THE SUSPECTS ARE MISS SCARLET (LINDA), MRS. WHITE (BOB), PROF. PLUM (TEDDY), MRS. PEACOCK (TINA), COL. MUSTARD (GENE), AND MR. GREEN (LOUISE).
what? THE POTENTIAL WEAPONS ARE THE BURGER PHONE, GENE'S KEYBOARD, TINA'S PORCELAIN HORSE HORCELAIN, A WINE BOTTLE, A SPATULA AND KUCHI KOPI.
where? THE ROOMS WHERE THIS COULD HAVE HAPPENED ARE THE KITCHEN, BOB & LINDA'S BEDROOM, BATHROOM, TINA'S BEDROOM, LIVING ROOM, GENE'S BEDROOM, STAIRCASE, LOUISE'S BEDROOM AND HALLWAY.
set-up. TO START THE GAME, PLACE ALL 6 WEAPONS IN THE CENTER OF THE BOARD.
PLACE EACH SUSPECT ON THEIR CORRESPONDING COLOR SQUARE AROUND THE BOARD.
1. GIVE EACH PLAYER:
clue sheet,
MOVER,
PEN (NOT INCLUDED).
2. SHUFFLE CARDS INTO 4 DECKS:
SUSPECTS
WEAPONS
LOCATIONS
RUMOR CARDS
WITHOUT LOOKING, PLACE ONE SUSPECT, ITEM, AND LOCATION CARD INTO THE MANILA ENVELOPE.
THESE ARE THE CARDS INVOLVED IN THE MYSTERY OF NED BODDY!
SHUFFLE THE REST OF THE CARDS TOGETHER, AND DEAL THEM EVENLY TO EACH PLAYER.
IF THERE ARE ANY LEFTOVERS, PLACE THEM FACE UP BY THE SIDE OF THE TABLE.
INTRIGUE CARDS
SHUFFLE THESE SEPARATELY AND PLACE THEM FACE DOWN NEXT TO THE GAME BOARD.
3. PLAYERS SECRETLY CROSS OFF THEIR CLUE CARDS ALONG WITH ANY LEFTOVER ONES ON THEIR CLUE SHEET. THROUGHOUT THE GAME YOU WILL SEE OTHER PLAYERS' CARDS, WHICH YOU MUST ALSO CROSS OFF TO DETERMINE WHICH CARDS ARE IN THE ENVELOPE AND INVOLVED IN THE MYSTERY OF NED BODDY.
GAME RULES
EVERYONE ROLLS THE DICE. HIGHEST ROLLER GOES FIRST, AND PLAY CONTINUES LEFT.
1. ON YOUR TURN, ROLL THE DICE AND MOVE AROUND THE BOARD. YOU MAY MOVE HORIZONTALLY AND VERTICALLY, FORWARD OR BACKWARD, BUT NEVER DIAGONALLY. YOU DON'T NEED AN EXACT ROLL TO ENTER A LOCATION, YOU MAY ALWAYS FINISH YOUR MOVE EARLY. (IF YOUR MOVER WAS MOVED BY ANOTHER PLAYER SINCE YOUR LAST TURN, YOU CAN CHOOSE TO STAY IN THAT LOCATION AND ASK A QUESTION. AND IF YOU'RE IN A CORNER ROOM, YOU CAN USE THE CRAWLSPACE SECRET PASSAGE TO ENTER THE ROOM IN THE OPPOSITE CORNER.)
2. UPON ENTERING A LOCATION, ASK A QUESTION ABOUT ONE SUSPECT, ONE WEAPON, AND THE ROOM YOU JUST ENTERED. BRING THE SUSPECT'S MOVER AND THE WEAPON INTO THE ROOM WITH YOU. THE PLAYER ON YOUR LEFT MUST TRY TO ANSWER YOU. IF THEY HAVE ANY OF THE CARDS IN QUESTION, THEY MUST REVEAL ONE TO YOU SECRETLY. IF THEY DON'T THEY SIMPLY SAY "I CANNOT ANSWER," AND THE QUESTION PASSES TO THE PLAYER ON THEIR LEFT, AND SO ON UNTIL YOU HAVE BEEN SHOWN ONE CARD.
3. END YOUR TURN BY CROSSING OFF THE CARD YOU'VE BEEN SHOWN ON YOUR CLUE SHEET. LEAVE THE SUSPECT MOVER AND ITEM YOU MOVED WHERE THEY ARE.
IF NO ONE CAN SHOW YOU A CARD, PUT ON A POKER FACE!
AS LONG AS YOU WEREN'T BLUFFING BY ASKING ABOUT ONE OF YOUR OWN CARDS, YOU'VE FIGURED OUT WHICH CARDS ARE IN THE ENVELOPE!
How To WIN
WHEN YOU THINK YOU'VE FIGURED OUT WHICH THREE CARDS ARE IN THE ENVELOPE, MAKE A MAD DASH TO THE CENTER SQUARE. ONCE YOU'RE THERE, YOU CAN MAKE AN ACCUSATION!
SAY YOUR ACCUSATION ALOUD: "I ACCUSE (SUSPECT) OF COMMITTING THE HEINOUS CRIME IN THE (ROOM) WITH THE (WEAPON)." THEN, DISCREETLY CHECK THE CARDS IN THE ENVELOPE.
IF YOU WERE RIGHT?
TAKE THE CARDS OUT OF THE ENVELOPE, SHOWING EVERYONE YOU'VE SOLVED THE MYSTERY AND WON THE GAME!
IF YOU WERE WRONG?
RETURN THE CARDS TO THE ENVELOPE.
YOU CANNOT WIN, AND YOU MAY ONLY ANSWER QUESTIONS FOR THE REMAINDER OF THE GAME.
IF EVERY PLAYER IS EVENTUALLY WRONG?
YOU ALL NEED A NAP. GO LIE DOWN AND PLAY AGAIN IN A FEW HOURS!
INTRIGUE CARDS
IF YOU LAND ON A QUESTION MARK, ROLL A QUESTION MARK, OR ARE MOVED INTO A LOCATION TO BE QUESTIONED, YOU MAY DRAW AN INTRIGUE CARD TO REVEAL A BONUS POWER. UNLESS THE POWER SAYS "PLAY THIS CARD IMMEDIATELY" YOU MAY CHOOSE WHEN TO PLAY IT. YOU CAN PLAY AS MANY CARDS AS YOU LIKE ON ANY TURN.
INTRIGUE CARDS MUST BE DISCARDED AFTER USE.
